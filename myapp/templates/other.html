01 How much will it cost to hire a lawyer for my case?

At BM Musyoki and Co. Advocates, our fees depend on the complexity and nature of your case. We offer a transparent fee structure, which may include hourly rates, flat fees, or contingency fees for specific types of cases. During your initial consultation, we will provide you with an estimate of the expected costs, ensuring there are no hidden fees.

02 Will my case go to trial, or can it be settled out of court?

Many cases can be resolved without going to trial through negotiation, mediation, or settlement. However, if a fair resolution cannot be reached, we are fully prepared to take your case to court. At BM Musyoki and Co. Advocates, we work closely with you to explore all options and advise you on the best course of action for your situation.

03 What should I bring to my initial consultation with a lawyer?

For your initial consultation at BM Musyoki and Co. Advocates, it's helpful to bring all relevant documents related to your case. This may include contracts, correspondence, court documents, or any other evidence you believe is important. Additionally, prepare a list of questions or concerns you have about your case. This will help us better understand your situation and provide you with the best possible advice.
